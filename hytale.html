HYTALE IS SAVED!

After years of silence, uncertainty, and fear that the project might never see the light of day,
Hytale has finally been saved - and not by just anyone. Simon, Hypixel owner and original creator of the project,
stepped in and bought it back from Riot Games, who wanted to shut the entire project down.

I've been waiting for Hytale ever since the very first announcement trailer.
Back then, the game felt like the next evolution of sandbox adventures: beautifully stylized worlds,
deep RPG mechanics, storytelling potential, modding capabilities, and all the creativity that Hypixel is known for.

Over the years, even with delays and silence, I never stopped believing in the vision behind Hytale.
Now knowing that Simon has taken control again gives me more hope than ever.
It feels like the heart of the project has been restored.

Now I'm waiting for it more than ever - excited, nostalgic, and genuinely inspired.
If Hytale finally releases under the guidance of its original creator, it might become the game
I always hoped it would be since that trailer dropped all those years ago.

Welcome back, Hytale. You survived.
